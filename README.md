# DORK — Vote Distribution Shape Analysis

Interactive visualization exploring whether winning songs in the DORK draft win on **passion** (a few people love it) or **consensus** (everyone thinks it's fine).

Covers DORK 2.0 (Rounds 1-3) and DORK 3.0 (Round 1).

**Live site:** https://kategage.github.io/DORK/

## What it shows

Each song's vote distribution is rendered as a stacked bar — darker blocks mean higher individual point values. Songs are tagged as:

- **Winner** — round winner
- **Passionate minority** — high intensity votes but lost on breadth
- **Consensus pick** — among the most-voted songs in the round
